Artist Spotlight


Sedef Adafi

Ausburg-based Sedef Adafi has built a reputation for herself as a local champion of the electronic music scene. She runs HAMAN NIGHTS, a female collective regularly hitting the decks at City Club with an enthralling mix of house and techno that's bound to get you sweating. Fueled on mate and armed with a crate of smooth, soulful vinyl, it's no surprise twenty-something Adafi has risen through the ranks to perform amongst some of Berlin, Hamburg and Amsterdam's finest DJs.
